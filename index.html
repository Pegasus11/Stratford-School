Auditorium

Upgraded in 2017

Stratford's auditorium is huge - enough to fit more than 50 people at a time! This auditorium also includes stage with curtains so that classes can perform plays throughout the year.

Library

Upgraded in 2017

This amazing library has a lot of books and is comfy too. Students get to check out a new book every month. Topics range from man-eating sharks to magical spinning treehouses.

Music Room

Built in 1998

This music room is where kids sing and play instrument together. There is a large variety of instruments such as the clarinet, bass drum, trombone, and violin. The amazing teacher, Ms. Kaygan, teaches kids all kinds of different and coool songs in this room.

what's new


  • New: Stratford Online Academy

    Stratford has offered an online version to kids who want to go online. They are using a website called Schoology to learn online. Teachers have been hired from all over the state. One teacher is in Georgia, another is in Florida. One could even be in New York. The schedule and the curriculum will stay the same as the in-person school.

  • New: Dust from Construction Site

    The location of Stratford Osgood is not well planned. Dust coming from an adjacent construction site can be harmful for the kids. It is a big issue that the princepal is working to resolve. Concerned parents have been complaining about the huge amounts dust and toxic emissions in the playground. Some parents are sending their kids with masks on and other parents are keeping their kids inside the building.
